  1. School Choir

    2. To bond students together with enthusiasm and a shared love and passion for music.
    The School Choir, the School Orchestra, the Percussion Band, and the Handbell Team are all teams under the supervision of the Music Association. All four teams have regular practices and internal meetings among officers. Besides, several interest classes including the newly established Wind band, percussion interest classes, and cello ensemble are under the management of the Music Association as well.
    The Music Association aims to bond students together with enthusiasm and a shared love and passion for music. With the common goal to achieve high standard musical performances, the four teams have participated in various musical events of every scholastic year like Music Festival, Lunch Concert, Christmas Flash Performance, Christmas Party Performance etc. Students have also taking part in various external events and competitions such as the Inter-School Singing Competition and the MYSO concerts.
